What is Puddleford?

Puddleford is a fictional English town that exists across time. The same cobbled streets, the same Dog and Duck, the same inexplicable events, visited in the 1690s, the 1930s, the far future, and everywhere in between.

Every episode is entirely improvised. There are no scripts, no rehearsals, no safety net. The Puddleford Players step up to the microphone and invent a town, its people, and its troubles from scratch.

Made with love (and a certain reckless abandon) by the Puddleford Players at MKTOC, Milton Keynes Theatre of Comedy.
