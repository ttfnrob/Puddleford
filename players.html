Milton Keynes Theatre of Comedy

The Puddleford Players are all members of Milton Keynes Theatre of Comedy (MKTOC), founded in 2003. MKTOC stages comedy productions, stage adaptations of well-known television comedies, murder mystery events, historical re-enactment, and improvisation shows. Puddleford is their entirely improvised podcast series.
