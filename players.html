The Puddleford Players

The improvisers behind the town.

"Everything you hear is totally made up, and improvised on the spot."
🎙️
Rob Simpson
Performer & Co-creator

Co-creator of the Puddleford universe and one of its most enthusiastic residents. Rob has a particular talent for conjuring Puddleford's more improbable civic institutions and the baffling characters who run them.

🎭
Sean McDermott
Performer

Sean brings a range of voices and an instinct for Puddleford's stranger corners. Whether it's the 1690s or the 2180s, Sean finds the human — and occasionally the inhuman — heart of every scene.

🃏
David Lovesy
Performer

David is the wildcard in Puddleford's deck. A gifted character improviser who can pivot from heartfelt to absurd in a single breath, and frequently does. The town would be much duller without him.

⭐
Vicki Simpson
Performer

Vicki grounds Puddleford's most outlandish moments with warmth and wit. An instinctive storyteller, she has a gift for finding the genuine emotion inside even the most ridiculous of Puddleford's predicaments.

🎪
Steve Clark
Performer

Steve is the steady hand and the wildly flailing one, sometimes simultaneously. His commitment to whatever Puddleford throws at him is total, and his timing is the envy of the town square.

About MKTOC

The Puddleford Players are part of MKTOC — Milton Keynes Theatre of Comedy — a community improv group based in Milton Keynes. MKTOC performs, teaches, and celebrates the art of improvised theatre.

Puddleford is their flagship audio project: an episodic, entirely improvised audio drama that has grown a town, a mythology, and a devoted listener base from nothing but imagination and a microphone.
