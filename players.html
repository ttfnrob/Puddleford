The Puddleford Players

The improvisers behind the town.

"Everything you hear is totally made up, and improvised on the spot by the Puddleford Players."
🎭
Rob Simpson
Co-creator of Puddleford and one of its founding inhabitants. Rob has a gift for giving fictional characters very specific opinions about very minor things. Also responsible for any good ideas.
🎭
Sean McDermott
A man who can commit fully to any character, no matter how inadvisable. Sean is responsible for a significant proportion of Puddleford's plot twists, none of which were planned.
🎭
David Lovesy
David brings warmth, wit, and a remarkable ability to say exactly the wrong thing at exactly the right time. Puddleford would be a quieter, less interesting place without him.
🎭
Vicki Simpson
Vicki is the backbone of many a Puddleford scene — grounded, funny, and frequently the only sensible person in the room (which, in an improvised drama, is not always an advantage).
🎭
Steve Clark
Steve specialises in characters who are absolutely certain they are right, even when history, logic, and basic common sense suggest otherwise. Puddleford is richer for it.

Milton Keynes Theatre of Comedy

The Puddleford Players are all members of MKTOC — Milton Keynes Theatre of Comedy — a community improv group based in Milton Keynes, UK. Puddleford is their flagship podcast project. They also perform live improv shows throughout the year.
