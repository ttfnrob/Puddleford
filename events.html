See the Town Come to Life

For the first time, the Puddleford Players will improvise the town live in front of an audience. No scripts. No safety net. Just Puddleford, happening in real time.

July
12

Milton Keynes Comedy Festival

The Residence Bar and Kitchen, Milton Keynes  ·  Sunday 12 July 2026, 1.30pm

The first ever live Puddleford show. The Puddleford Players take to the stage at the Milton Keynes Comedy Festival to improvise the town in front of a live audience for the very first time. Every character, every twist, every scandal, made up on the spot, in the room, with you there to witness it. Tickets are eight pounds. MKTOC are also staging two further shows at the same venue that day: Pitch Slapped at 4.30pm and Whose Turn? at 7.30pm.

What to Expect

No Script

Nothing is written in advance. Not a word. The Puddleford Players arrive with nothing but each other and the accumulated lore of the town.

Anything Can Happen

Puddleford has hosted pirates, time travellers, and a flying castle. The live show will be no different. Come prepared for the unexpected.

You Are Part of It

A live audience changes everything. The town comes alive differently when it has people in the room. You are not just watching. You are witnessing a Puddleford that has never existed before.

Puddleford

An entirely improvised episodic audio drama about a fictional English town.
