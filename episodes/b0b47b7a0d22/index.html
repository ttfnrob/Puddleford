The Wind Knew His Name: The Cobb Thomas Story

10 July 2026 · 00:37:29

Every episode of Puddleford is entirely improvised on the spot by the Puddleford Players. We add sound effects and music during the edit - just for fun.


This week: We've all heard his songs - but now the Puddleford Players can finally bring you the real unvarnished tale of Thomas Cobb.


The musical genius is unmasked in this truth-telling, original biopic.


Narrated by Vicki

Players: David, Sean, Jodie, Finley, Avalon (on guitar too!) and Gaia
