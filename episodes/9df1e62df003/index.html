Season 2

The Cabernet Cove Mysteries

17 July 2026 · 00:47:18

Every episode of Puddleford is entirely improvised on the spot by the Puddleford Players. We add sound effects and music during the edit - just for fun.


This week: Jennifer Fincher is a local author and amateur sleuth around the twee corner of Puddleford known as Cabernet Cove. She seems to know everyone, especially the local police department. When a local man is murdered she is ready to work out who is responsible.


Biscuit?


Narrated by Rob

this week's players are Vicki, Finley, Sean, David and Gaia.
